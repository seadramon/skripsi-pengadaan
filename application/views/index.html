Tentang SCM Summit

SCM Summit is a showcase of Oil & Gas SCM Society to the SCM Professionals and Business Leaders by bringing an update knowledge and expertise about business, system and people in the dynamic market place nowadays. In addition, the event is served as place for SCM Professional and Indonesia Business leaders to discuss and share about what is happening and changes in the business and market place and learn new method and innovative approach related to people and business solutions.

This event this year is partnered by SKKMIGAS, BP and Petronas, together with speakers from leading business practitioners and the world’s acknowledge experts in the field of SCM. Indonesia SCM Summit 2014 sets out to portray not simply how to achieve successful SCM, but how to identify of lessions learned made along the way, and to challenge some of misconceptions and myths that surround them.

Attending the event will provide you an opportunity to get questions answered, as well as presenting you with a unique chance to discuss your challenged with experts, colleagues and counterparts. We look forward to meeting you at SCM Summit 2015 and engaging with you in a productive discussion that will add measurable value to your business.
